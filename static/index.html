Tutorial

1. Use Chrome, sign into my.bham, then go to my timetables, find your timetable for this week.

  • Right click the timetable.
  • Click 'View frame source'

2. Right click the source codes, then click 'Save as...'

3. Upload the saved file (.html), then you'll get your timetable.
